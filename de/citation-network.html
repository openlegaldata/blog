Citation Network of German Court Decisions

Explore citation patterns of court decisions with data provided by Open Legal Data. Which jurisdictions cites what laws and decisions? Click on nodes to see details. Zoom and drag graph with mouse.

  • Nodes: Law codes and court decisions
  • Edges: Citations
  • Colors: Court Jurisdiction, Laws = green
  • Dataset: here (limited to 50,000 edges)
  • Graph processing: Gephi + JSON exporter
